Introduction

What

Don't let life pass you by. 365 is a mobile application designed to help you break out of the daily grind so you can take a step back, reflect, and remember today forever.

Why

Today is unique. Today is special. And today is going to disappear soon. Don't you want to remember it?

How

Social Journaling. Your day is special not only because of the novel experiences you've had, but because of the experiences of your friends and people around you.

“Write it on your heart that every day is the best day in the year.” ― Ralph Waldo Emerson

Application

“Forever is composed of nows.” ― Emily Dickinson

Team

All three of us keep journals and write in them every day. We've seen the benefits that journaling has brought to our lives and we want to bring that to the world. Our mission is to help people be more aware and mindful of the novel experiences they have every day. We want to help you realize why every day of your life is special, important, and most of all: worth remembering
